Services

Global Freight Forwarding

Transglobal Container Lines provides a wide range of sea freight forwarding services including our core business of NVOCC operator, project cargo, transshipment, consolidation services for less than container load (LCL) & full container load (FCL) with a Worldwide network of partners who are leaders in their respective areas.

 

Warehousing & Transportation

Whether you need short-term regional storage services or long-term warehousing, order fulfillment, logistics and distribution solutions, we have the facilities and expertise to help. We can also organize the delivery to any storage.

 

Transglobal Container Lines has a professional Bonded Cargo handling facility to manage all transshipment and local cargoes moving globally, at competitive freight rates.

 

We also provide services such as :

  • Re-packing

  • Marking

  • Labeling / Re-Labeling

  • Inkjet Printing on cartons

  • Palletization

  • Cross Stuffing of Containers

Container Trading

Not merely that we trade containers at competitive prices, we provide value added services on case by case basis to mobilize the containers to the required destinations.
